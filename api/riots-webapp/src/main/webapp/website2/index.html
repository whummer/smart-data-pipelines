What is riots?

Riots is creating a next generation platform for rapid development of Internet of Things (IoT) applications. The riots product suite allows users to gain new insights on which "things" (or devices) are available in the market, how applications and people consume devices, how devices are configured, interconnected, operated, and maintained. Given the predicted scale of IoT solutions and the number of target industry verticals (buildings, healthcare, and infrastructure, to name just a few), this is critical data for a broader ecosystem of device manufacturers, end-users, developers, integrators, and third-party service providers.

Find and Integrate the Right Devices

Search and find suitable "things" and devices in a comprehensive catalog. Manufacturers can maintain detailed specifications of their devices.

Define and Provision Virtual Sensors

No need to purchase equipment or set up expensive lab environments or field tests to develop and verify your application logic. Choose from hundreds of virtualized sensors or create your own.

Simulations and Robustness Testing

Things can go wrong. And they probably will. Using our robustness testing module, you can make sure your application can gracefully handle sensor failures, quality degradations, or spurious data. Additionally, data and interaction traces are captured from your infrastructure and can be played back to you for testing.
